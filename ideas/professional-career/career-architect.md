# Career Architect

## Use Case

Long-term career planning with opportunity evaluation, skill gap analysis, and professional narrative development. Move from reactive job searching to intentional career building with clear vision and strategy.

## Execution Difficulty

**Level**: Medium

**Rationale**: Requires thoughtful self-assessment and ongoing maintenance, but the structure enables rather than complicates.

## Suggested Folder Structure

```
├── profile/
│   ├── experience.md            # Career history
│   ├── skills.md                # Skills inventory
│   └── achievements.md          # Key accomplishments
├── vision/
│   ├── 1-year.md
│   ├── 5-year.md
│   └── ideal-role.md
├── opportunities/
│   ├── evaluating/              # Being considered
│   └── archived/                # Past opportunities
├── gaps/
│   └── skills.md                # Gaps to address
└── outputs/
    ├── narratives/              # Career story versions
    └── strategies/              # Career strategies
```

## Key Workflows

- Define career vision at multiple time horizons
- Evaluate opportunities against vision and values
- Identify skill and experience gaps with closure plans
- Develop and refine career narratives for different audiences
- Periodic career review and strategy adjustment

## Suggested Slash Commands

| Command | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `/evaluate-opportunity` | Analyze opportunity against vision and gaps |
| `/identify-gaps` | Compare current state to vision, find gaps |
| `/update-narrative` | Refine career story for specific audience |
| `/annual-review` | Comprehensive career review and strategy update |

## Suggested Agents

| Agent | Role |
|-------|------|
| `opportunity-analyst` | Evaluates opportunities against defined criteria and vision |

## Why This Works as a Claude Space

- Career development spans years
- Opportunity evaluation benefits from consistent criteria
- Narratives improve through iteration and feedback
